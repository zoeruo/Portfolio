Undefined Senses

Undefined Senses involved 12 participants per play. By the intervention of messages, sounds, videos, and other new media forms, the audience members were separated from their normal status and then re-entered the city. The audience experienced a 120 minutes action, passing through or staying in different city areas.

The Window

The Window is an installation artwork glowing in the nighttime. We used Arduino to control the pwm signal.
