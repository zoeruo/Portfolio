The Window

Installation Art

Overview

In the daytime, The Window is no different from other windows. Serving as an exit of our visions, we can see through it clearly. Nonetheless, when it comes to nighttime, the lights are lit, and it loses its penetration characteristics. The Window merges the city lights into a glossy surface, which hides the invisible emptiness behind it.

The Window is an installation artwork glowing in the nighttime. We used Arduino to control the pwm signal.

Role

Date

Creating Process

To make the three meters tall installation stand in the wind, we sought advice from an iron factory professional. To make it stand in the rain, we applied waterproof measures. We laser cut small dots on Acrylic to make the whole area shine, and we used Arduino to control the light. Sitting in front of The Window, I solely defined the lighting styles and wrote the codes.
